Goal

Combine local merchant deals with casual-game rewards to drive engagement and coupon redemption.

Project Overview

GlamBrandi - Cascade is an innovative platform that combines e-commerce with gamification to create an engaging consumer experience. The platform connects users with local merchants through a web-based consumer dashboard where they can access exclusive discounts and promotional offers.

View Nearby Deals Explore Games Open Coupon Wallet

Consumer Dashboard

The consumer dashboard serves as a digital marketplace where users can:

  • Discover daily discounted coupons

    Log in to find fresh offers from merchants in your vicinity.

  • Access location-based deals

    Browse promotions from participating local businesses.

  • Redeem earned coupons at merchants

    Use coupons you’ve earned (including through gameplay) at onboarded locations.

Gamification Component

The platform features proprietary casual games developed by Innovative Configuration, designed to increase user engagement and reward participation. Users can play these games to earn redeemable coupons for use at partner merchant stores.

Current Game Portfolio

Slot Machine, BlackJack, CardMatch, Roulette (Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS).
